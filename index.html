Al Mubasheri
Advocates & Legal Consultancy has grown to become one of the finest providers of legal services in the UAE, with the ultimate goal of offering exceptional legal support through exploring all available and inventive approaches where clients' best interests are our top priority.

Our Vision

Perseverance, professionalism and dedication to our clients constitute our core values. These values provide the foundation to be recognized as the leading law firm in UAE and the GCC region. We pursue continuous growth within domestic and regional markets while instilling a culture that embraces good governance, diligence, integrity and transparency.
